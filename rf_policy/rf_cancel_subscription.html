Cancel Subscription Instructions for Read Fast

This link will guide you through the process of cenceling your subscription for Read Fast.

Follow these steps to cancel the subscription:

  1. Open the Read Fast on your device.
  2. Look for the menu button, usually located in the top-left corner, and tap it to open the drawer.
  3. Locate and select the option that says "Settings"
  4. You will be redirected to the play store to manage your subscription
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm and complete the cancelation process.

Note: Account deletion will result in not beign able to use or access to Read Fast features
